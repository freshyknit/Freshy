Editing

The more you practice, edit and experiment, the more you will become pro. Practice is the key. They may look hard in the beginning, but after 15 days to 1 month practice, you will love them doing.

Watch and practice simultaneously 1 video only in 1 day and after watching that video, practice the things what you learnt from it

The given sources may look like very large but they are worth its time.

FOR: High Specs Laptops | Low Specs Laptops | Mobile


Vector Photo Editing

Adobe Illustrator | Inkscape

Vectors are scalable images that can be sized as small or as large are you need them to be, but look the same when it comes to clarity and resolution.
Great for making Icons, Logos, Posters and Graphics


Raster Photo Editing

Adobe Photoshop | Photopea

Photoshop is raster-based and uses pixels to create images.
Great for photo editing, making posters, advertisements, wallpapers and digital artwork.
Based on pixels while Illustrator works using vectors.


Video Editing

Adobe Premiere | Filmora | Kinemaster

Vectors are scalable images that can be sized as small or as large are you need them to be, but look the same when it comes to clarity and resolution and store at mathematical equations
Great for making Icons, Logos, Posters and Graphics


Motion Graphics

Adobe After-Effects | Natron

For creating all kinds of visual effects.
Commonly used for creating Animation, motion-graphics and other cinematic effects


Audio Editing

Adobe Audition | Audacity

Audio editing software design to record, edit and mix Audio
